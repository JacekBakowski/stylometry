brahmaṇi pravilīyate ghaṭe naṣṭe yathā vyoma vyomā eva bhavati sphuṭam tathā eva upādhi vilaye brahmā eva brahma vid su ayam kṣīram kṣīre yathā kṣiptam tailam taile jalam jale saṃyuktam eka tām yāti tathā ātmani ātma vid muniḥ evam videha kaivalyam sat mātra tvam akhaṇḍitam brahma bhāvam prapadya eṣa yatiḥ na āvartate punar sat ātma ekatva vijñāna dagdha avidyā ādi varṣmaṇaḥ amuṣya brahma bhūta tvāt brahmaṇaḥ kutas udbhavaḥ māyā klṛptau bandha mokṣau na staḥ sva ātmani vastutas yathā rajjau niṣkriyāyām sarpa ābhāsa vinirgamau āvṛteḥ sadā asattvābhyām vaktavye bandha mokṣaṇe na āvṛtiḥ brahmaṇaḥ kācid anyā bhāvāt anāvṛtam yadi asti advaita hāniḥ syāt dvaitam naḥ sahate śrutiḥ bandhaḥ ca mokṣaḥ ca mṛṣā eva mūḍhāḥ buddheḥ guṇam vastuni kalpayanti dṛś āvṛtim megha kṛtām yathā ravau yatas advaya asaṅga cit ekam akṣaram asti iti pratyayaḥ yaḥ ca yaḥ ca na asti iti vastuni buddheḥ eva guṇau  etau na tu nityasya vastunaḥ atas tau māyayā klṛptau bandha mokṣau na ca ātmani niṣkale niṣkriye śānte niravadye nirañjane advitīye pare tattve vyoma vat kalpanā kutas na nirodhaḥ na ca utpatti rna bandhaḥ na ca sādhakaḥ na mumukṣuḥ na vai muktaḥ iti eṣā paramārtha tā sakala nigama cūḍā su ānta siddhānta guhyam param idam ati guhyam darśitam te mayā adya apagata kali doṣaḥ kāma nirmukta buddhi stat atulam asakṛt tvam bhāvayedam mumukṣuḥ iti śrutvā guroḥ vākyam praśrayeṇa kṛta ānatiḥ sa tena samanujñātaḥ yayau nirmukta bandhanaḥ guruḥ eṣa sadānanda sindhau nirmagna mānasaḥ pāvayan vasudhām sarvām vicacāra nirantaraḥ iti ācāryasya śiṣyasya saṃvādena ātma lakṣaṇam nirūpitam mumukṣūṇām sukha bodha upapattaye hitam idam upadeśam ādriyantām vihita nirasta samasta citta doṣāḥ bhava sukha viratāḥ praśānta cittāḥ śruti rasikāḥ yatayaḥ mumukṣavaḥ ye saṃsāra adhvani tāpa bhānu
