kiñca na kevalam anuṣṭhāna mātram asya prādhvam bandhanam ātmā ekatva rasa prajña tām sthirām bāgha eṣaṇa avyāvṛtta rūpām sarva sannyāsa lakṣaṇām tat hi bandhanam viduṣaḥ brahmāṇi evam hi baddhaḥ brahmāṇi saṃsāra abhimukhaḥ na āvartate tasmāt bandhanam ca asya sat ācaret tad anuṣṭhāna bandhane sat ācarataḥ kim syāt iti ucyate durdarśa duḥkhena hi eṣaṇā tyāga ādinā sa dṛśyate iti durdarśam nipuṇam yasmāt hi durdarśa tasmāt nipuṇam atyanta kauśalena samāhita cetasā yuktaḥ yaḥ paśyet sākṣāt upalabheta aham ātmā iti sa modeta evam dṛṣṭvā harṣam ānanda lakṣaṇam prāpnuyāt viṣṭape vigata santāpa lakṣaṇe asmin brahmaṇi iti arthaḥ ātman paśyan sarva bhūtāni na muhyet cintayan kaviḥ ātmānam ca eva sarvatra yaḥ paśyet sa vai brahmā nāka pṛṣṭhe virājati kiñca ātman paśyan ātmani paśyan upalabhamānaḥ sarva bhūtāni sarvāṇi bhūtāni sarveṣām bhūtānām ātma svarūpa tām eva paśyan= iti arthaḥ sarvatra ātmānam ca param na muhyet moham na gacchet na hi ātmā ekatva darśinaḥ moha avatāraḥ tatra kaḥ mohaḥ iti ca mantra liṅgāt kīdṛś viśiṣṭam ātma darśanam moha nibarhaṇam iti āha cintayan upasaṃhṛta karaṇaḥ kaviḥ medhāvī san dhyāyamānaḥ na śabda janita darśana mātreṇa moha apagamaḥ sarva bhūteṣu anupraviṣṭam ekam saṃvyavahāra kāle yaḥ hi yuktaḥ paśyet sa vai brahmā brāhmaṇaḥ nāka pṛṣṭhe suka rāgau brahmaṇi virājati vividham dīpyate nipuṇaḥ aṇīyān bisa ūrṇāyāyaḥ sarvam āvṛtya tiṣṭhati varṣīyām̐ḥ ca pṛthivyāḥ dhruvaḥ sarvam ārabhya tiṣṭhati saḥ indriyaiḥ jagataḥ asya jñānāda nyaḥ ananyasya jñeyāt parameṣṭhī vibhājaḥ tasmāt kāyāḥ prabhavanti sarve sa mūlam śāśvatikaḥ sa nityaḥ kiñca nipuṇaḥ sarva vid aṇīyān aṇutaraḥ bisa ūrṇāḥ yāḥ bisa tantoḥ api kaḥ asau yaḥ prakṛtaḥ ātmā sarva samastam jagat āvṛtya saṃvyāpya tiṣṭhati kiñca varṣīyān vṛddhataraḥ sthūlataraḥ ca pṛthivyāḥ sarva ātmakaḥ hi saḥ dhruvaḥ nityaḥ sarva kṛsnam ārabhya saṃstambhanam kṛtvā tiṣṭhati vartate yena dyauḥ ugrā pṛthivī ca dṛhā iti mantra liṅgāt sa sarva īśvaraḥ sarvajñaḥ ekaḥ vijñeyaḥ iti arthaḥ sa paramātmā indriyaiḥ janyate yat jñānam jagataḥ asya tasma ajñānāt anyaḥ vilakṣaṇaḥ laukika jñānāt anyaḥ iti viśeṣaṇa ajñāna ātmakaḥ iti etat siddham satyam jñānam anantam iti ca śruteḥ asya jagataḥ indriya janya jñānāt anyaḥ iti uktam atas ca tad vyatiriktam jagat iti prāptam atas tat mā bhūt iti āha ananyasya apṛthak bhūtasya jagataḥ jñayāt jñātavyāt parama artha svarūpā dvayāt parameśvarāt ghaṭādevi mṛdaḥ sa ca parameṣṭhī parame prakṛṣṭa khe mahimni hadi ākāśe avasthātum śīlam asya iti parameṣṭhī svayam eva vibhājaḥ vibhaktaḥ deva pitṛ manuṣya ādinā jñātṛ jñeya jñāna bhedena ca yasmāt sa eva jñeyaḥ ātmā svatas vibhajati jagat anekadhā tasmāt eva ātmanaḥ kāyāḥ śarīrāṇi ākāśa ādi krameṇa prabhavanti sarve brahma ādi lakṣaṇāḥ atas mūlam sa jagataḥ yatas vai imāni bhūtāni jāyante iti śruteḥ atas eva sa śāśvatikaḥ yaḥ hi pṛthivī ādi vikāraḥ saḥ abādi krameṇa vinaśyet param mūla kāraṇam āpadyate saḥ aśāśvatikaḥ anityaḥ ayam ca ātmā param mūlam na tasya api anyat mūlam asti yatas jātaḥ vinaśyet mūlam āpadyate tatas tad vilakṣaṇa tvāt śāśvati kaḥ śaśvat eka rūpaḥ atas nityaḥ eka tva mahat tva mūla tvebhyaḥ ca evam yathoktam ātmānam viditavataḥ ādhyātmikāḥ yogāḥ nyāya sahitāḥ apratibandhena bhaviṣyanti mithyā pratyaya pūrvakāḥ hi doṣāḥ doṣa nimittaḥ ca dharma adharma janitaḥ saṃsāraḥ doṣa nivṛttau= atyantam vinivartate iti etam artha darśayiṣyan= āha doṣāṇām tu nirghātaḥ yoga mūlaḥ iha jīvite nihatya bhūta dāhān kṣemam gacchati paṇḍitaḥ doṣāṇām tu krodha ādīnām nirghātaḥ vināśaḥ yogāḥ a krodha ādayaḥ tad mūlaḥ tad nimittaḥ iti etat akrodha ādiṣu hi satsu pratidvandvinaḥ doṣāḥ durbala tvāt nirhanyante iha jīvitaḥ iti doṣa prabhava karma nimitta tvāt jīvitasya deha dhāraṇa avasānaḥ doṣa vyāpāraḥ iti etat darśayati tad pratipakṣeṣu akrodha ādiṣu kathat nu nāma hīyān iti madrita kośapāḥ mumukṣavaḥ prayatna atiśayam kuryuḥ iti yoga doṣayoḥ itaretara virodhi tve sati sthiti gati vat yogebhyaḥ doṣāṇām eva nirghātaḥ na punar viparyayaḥ iti etat katham iti ced ucyate samyak darśana saciva tvāt balavantaḥ yogāḥ mithyā pratyaya saciva tvāt durbala tvāt nihanyante nihanti iti etat api uktam buddhi balavat bhayaḥ tad hīnānām loke nirdhataḥ dṛṣṭaḥ akrodhanaḥ krodha ādīn= ca iti liṅgāt nirhatya aparhatya bhūta dāhān doṣeṣu hi udbhūteṣu bhūtāni dahyante iva agninā paritapyante atas bhūta dāhāḥ doṣāḥ ucyante tān nirhatya kṣemam nirbhayam mokṣam gacchati ānandam brahmaṇaḥ vidvān na bibheti kutaścana abhayam vai janakaḥ ! prāptaḥ asi na bhavati viduṣām tatas bhayam ityādi śruti smṛtibhyaḥ na doṣa praśama mātreṇa abrahma vidaḥ kṣema prāptiḥ iti āha paṇḍitaḥ iti brahma vidi hi atra paṇḍita śabdaḥ prayuktaḥ na śāstra vidi tasmāt brāhmaṇaḥ pāṇḍityam nirvidyaḥ it śruteḥ iha ātma vidyā adhikārāt yadi tarhi doṣa nirharaṇam paṇḍitaḥ api apekṣeta karmāṇi phala dānāya pravṛttāni apravṛttāni ca yat tu pravṛttam karma tena ākṣiptāḥ doṣāḥ kartuḥ sukha duḥkha ādi phala dānāya doṣa abhāve phala ārambhaka tva anupapatteḥ na hi rāga dveṣa ādi śūnye sukha duḥkhe pravṛtti labdhiḥ kadācid kasyacid iha dṛśyate tasmāt phala dānāya pravṛttena karmaṇā ākṣiptāḥ doṣāḥ prasaṅgena prāpta balāḥ yatnataḥ nirhartavyāḥ pravṛtti ādhikya hetu tva prasaṅgāt ata
