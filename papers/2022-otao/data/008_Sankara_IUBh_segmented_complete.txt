ātmanaḥ prāpti dvāram yācate ahiraṇmayena pātreṇa iti hiraṇmayam iva hiraṇmayam jyotirmayam iti etad tena pātreṇa iva apidhāna bhūtena satyasya eva āditya maṇḍalasthasya brāhmaṇaḥ apihitam ācchāditam mukham dvāram tad tvam he pūṣan apāvṛṇu apasāraya satyasya upāsanāt satyam dharmaḥ yasya mama saḥ aham satyadharmā tasmai mahyam athavā yathā bhūtasya dharmasya anuṣṭhātre dṛṣṭaye tava satya ātmanaḥ upalabdhaye he pūṣan jagataḥ poṣaṇāt pūṣā raviḥ tathā ekaḥ eva ṛṣati gacchati iti ekarṣiḥ he ekarṣe tathā sarvasya saṃyamanāt yamaḥ he yama tathā raśmīnām prāṇānām rasānān ca svīkaraṇāt sūryaḥ he sūrya prajāpateḥ apatyam prājāpatyaḥ he prājāpatya vyūha vigamaya raśmīn svān samūha ekīkuru upasaṃharate tejaḥ tāpakam jyotiḥ yad te tava rūpam kalyāṇatamam atyanta śobhanam tad te tava ātmanaḥ prasādāt paśyāmi kiṃca aham na tu tvām bhṛtyavat yāce yaḥ asau āditya maṇḍala sthaḥ vyāhṛtya vayavaḥ puruṣaḥ puruṣa ākāratvāt pūrṇam vānena prāṇa buddhayā ātmanā jagat samastam iti puruṣaḥ puri śayanāt vā puruṣaḥ saḥ aham asmi bhavāmi atha idānīm mama mariṣyataḥ vāyuḥ prāṇaḥ adhyātma paricchedam hitvā adhidaivatā ātmānam sarva ātmakam anilam amṛtam sūtrātmānam pratipadyatām iti vākya śeṣaḥ liṅgam ca idam jñāna karman saṃskṛtam utkrāmatu iti draṣṭavyam mārga yācana sāmarthyāt atha idam śarīram agnau hutam bhasmāntam bhūyāt om iti yathā upāsanam om pratīka ātmakatvāt satya ātmakam agni ākhyam brahman abhedena ucyate he krato saṅkalpa ātmaka smara yad mama smartavyam tasya kālaḥ ayam pratyupasthitaḥ ataḥ smara etāvantam kālam bhāvitam kṛtam agne smara yad mayā bālya prabhṛti anuṣṭhitam karman tad ca smara krataḥ smara kṛtam smara iti punar vacanam ādara artham punar anyena mantreṇa mārgam yācate he agne naya gamaya supathā śobhanena mārgeṇa supathā iti viśeṣaṇam dakṣiṇa mārga nivṛtti artham nirviṇṇaḥ aham dakṣiṇena mārgeṇa gata agata lakṣaṇena ataḥ yāce tvām punaḥ punar gamana agamana varjitena śobhanena pathā naya rāye dhanāya karman phala bhogāya iti arthaḥ asmān yathā ukta dharma phala viśiṣṭān viśvāni sarvāṇi he deva vayunāni karmāṇi prajñānāni vā vidvān jānan kiṃca yuyodhi viyojaya vināśaya asmat asmat taḥ juhurāṇam kuṭilam vañcana ātmakam enaḥ pāpam tataḥ vayam viśuddhāḥ santa iṣṭam prāpsyāmaḥ iti abhiprāyaḥ kim tu vayam idānīm te na śaknumaḥ paricaryām kartum bhūyiṣṭhām bahutarām te tubhyam nama uktim namaskāra vacanam vidhema namaskāreṇa paricarema iti arthaḥ iti śrutvā kecid saṃśayam kurvanti ataḥ tad nirākaraṇa artham saṃkṣepataḥ vicāraṇām kariṣyāmaḥ tatra tāvat kim nimittaḥ saṃśaya iti ucyate vidyā śabdena mukhyā paramātma vidyā eva kasmāt na gṛhyate amṛtatvam ca nānu uktāyāḥ paramātma vidyāyāḥ karmaṇaḥ ca virodhāt samuccayān upapattiḥ satyam virodhaḥ tu na avagamyate virodha avirodhayoḥ śāstra pramāṇakatvāt yathā avidyā anuṣṭhānam vidyā upāsanam ca śāstra pramāṇakam tathā tad virodha avirodhau api yathā ca na hiṃsyāt sarvā bhūtāni iti śāstrāt avagatam punaḥ śāstreṇa eva bādhyate iti evam vidyā avidyayoḥ api syāt vidyā karmaṇoḥ ca samuccayaḥ na iti śruteḥ iti vacanāt avirodha iti ced na hetu svarūpa phala virodhāt vidyā avidyā virodha avirodhayoḥ vikalpa asambhavāt samuccaya vidhānāt avirodhaḥ eva iti ced na saha sambhava anupapatteḥ krameṇa ekāśraye syātām vidyā avidye iti ced na vidyā utpattau avidyāyāḥ hyaḥ tatvāt tad āśraye avidyā anupapatteḥ na hi agniḥ uṣṇaḥ prakāśaḥ ca iti vijñāna utpattau yasmin na āśraye tad utpannam tasmin na iva āśraye śītaḥ agniḥ aprakāśaḥ veti avidyāyāḥ utpattiḥ na api saṃśayaḥ ajñānam vā iti śoka moha ādya sambhava śruteḥ avidyā asambhavāt tad upādānasya karmaṇaḥ api anupapattim avocāma amṛtam aśnuta iti āpekṣikam amṛtam vidyā śabdena paramātma vidyā grahaṇe hiraṇmayena iti ādinā dvāra mārga ādi yācanam anupapannam syāt tasmāt upāsanayā samuccayaḥ na paramātma vijñānena iti
