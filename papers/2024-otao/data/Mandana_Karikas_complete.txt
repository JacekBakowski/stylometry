katham adṛṣṭa tvāt apravṛttiḥ śuktikā śakale samāḥ dṛṣṭam tad yena rūpeṇa tad pravṛtteḥ akāraṇam dṛṣṭa smṛtā vivekāt ced idam atra parīkṣyatām tattva bodhāt atha atattva abodhāt rajata vedanāt dṛṣṭe pravṛttiḥ pūrvasmin viparīta artha tā mateḥ na dṛṣṭa adṛṣṭayoḥ bhedaḥ parasminna upayoginī sva yoga darśane te hi samāropa upayoginī na adṛṣṭe asaṃprayukte vā cākṣuṣaḥ syāt viparyayaḥ smṛtam pratyakṣataḥ bhinnam sva jñānāt eva ced kutas avivekaḥ anyathā tu syāt sadā sāmānya darśane viparyayaḥ anyam smarataḥ na ca saṃśaya darśanāt  smṛta dvaya aviveka uttham saṃśayam yadi manyate tad na ubhayoḥ api yatas smṛtau dṛṣṭaḥ viparyayaḥ viśeṣa jñānataḥ adhyakṣe smṛtam yadi vivicyate na sva jñānāt asati asmin dvi smṛtau katham eka dhīḥ eka tvam eva smarataḥ prāgrūpam śaśini sphuṭam timira ādi pradoṣeṇa katham te dvi tva vibhramaḥ pipāsataḥ ca salilam śuktikā hita cetasaḥ viparyayaḥ na hi bhavet bhavan vā salile bhavet na ca sarvāḥ niyogena bhrāntiḥ sādṛśya bandhanā śvete pīta bhramaḥ dṛṣṭaḥ madhure tikta vibhramaḥ kiñcid sādṛśyataḥ hi syāt na kaścid tatra na bhramaḥ tasmāt indriya doṣāṇām sāmarthyasya vibhāgataḥ bhrameṣu niyamaḥ doṣāt agrahe na bhrame yamaḥ viśeṣa smaraṇāt na api smṛtau  api tad agrahaḥ deśa kāla viśeṣāt ca na smṛtam pravivicyate aneka deśa adhigatam viviktam na punar tatas smṛtiḥ iti api vijñānam smṛteḥ anyat udāhṛtam na ca māna phalāt bhinnāt tad siddhi ati phalāt ṛte sati smṛti viveke ca pratyabhijñāna vibhramaḥ atha ana saṃnihitaḥ vyarthaḥ vedyaḥ saṃnidhimat tayā avivikta smṛteḥ evam viparīta artha tā bhavet manasaḥ anupaghātāt ca na asaṃnidhya parigrahaḥ doṣaḥ hi agrahaṇe hetuḥ indriyāṇām udāhṛtaḥ pravṛtti niyamaḥ na syāt iti ca atra niveditam sarva jñānāni mithyā ca prasajyante atra kalpane sarva ātmanā arthaḥ  jñānena kenacid na hi gṛhyate tathā ajñāta vivekasya dvi candra ādi viparyayāt anuvṛttimataḥ paśyan kasya sva ātma apanihnute prasakta pratiṣedha ātmā khyātiḥ prāptau prakalpate na agrahaḥ prāpakaḥ abhāvaḥ prāpikā viparīta dhīḥ ye ca khyātī tu rajataḥ cakṣuḥ saṃyukta vastunoḥ na ayam tad upanīta artha pratiṣedhaḥ avagamyate na ca agrahaṇam eva eṣā pratītiḥ apabādhate sarvā hi agraha bādhena janma na syāt tathā īdṛśī viveka vijñānam idam na prasakta niṣedha dhīḥ iti bruvāṇaḥ vaiyātyāt svām pratītim apahnute na krame yaugapadye vā viveka matiḥ īdṛśī prāpte syāt aikya saṃvittau na bhedasya anirūpaṇe rajatasya smaran  cakṣuḥ saṃyuktasya ca vastunaḥ sāmānya dṛṣṭyā rajatāt abuddhvā ca viviktatām paścāt viviñcan pratyeti na evam sāmānya darśane koṭi dvaya smṛti abhāve saṃśayaḥ  na niyogataḥ viveka grahaṇa abhāvāt kāla saṃnidhi saṃvidaḥ tadvat ābhāsanāt prāpta āviṣṭaḥ syāt anyathā grahaḥ tatra agrahaḥ nimittam syāt athavā saṃnidhi grahaḥ bheda graha apavādena rahitaḥ bhāva rūpataḥ viparyaya anabhyupāye bhramaḥ agraha nibandhanaḥ śabda eka gamya uktaḥ syāt agrahaḥ agraha bandhanaḥ yaḥ pratīti virodhaḥ tu sa sva doṣaḥ udāhṛtaḥ sāmānādhikaraṇyena bodhāt rūpyam idam mitam anāśvāsaḥ  jñāyamāne jñānena eva apabādhyate vyabhicārāt asāmarthyam na tad kāryasya lābhataḥ vyabhicārāt prameya tve kāryam eva na labhyate api ca agrahaṇe abhīṣṭam yad viveka nibandhanam na parāṇudyate daṇḍaiḥ tad viparyaya darśane na udreka hetavaḥ doṣāḥ kārya sāmarthya ghātinaḥ graha āgraha vibhāgaḥ syāt atas tad sat asattvataḥ viparyaye hi nitarām upaghātaḥ prakalpate iṣṭa kārya uparodhena viparīta udayena ca tasmāt tad sat asat tvābhyām vivekaḥ atra api kalpate doṣe asati graha āśaṅkā katham ca vinivartate na hi kāraṇa sadbhāve kārya sattā niyogataḥ pratyakṣam kāryam evam ca hetunā anumitam bhavet viparyaye phala abhāvaḥ hetu abhāvāt tu yujyate anālamba natā pattiḥ yathā ākāra antara arpaṇam svarūpa anarpaṇāt evam bhavet agrahaṇe api te kasyacid tu arpaṇam tulyam atha tatra na tad tathā parasya api hi tatra evam atha tatra tathā sthiteḥ sarvatra syāt tathā bhāvaḥ syāt evam grahaṇe api te yathā tatra animitta tvāt samya jñāne animitta tā śaṅkyate grahaṇe api evam agrahe sā nirūpitā jñānāt eva nimitta tvam anyeṣām na viśiṣyate avaghāta prokṣaṇa ādeḥ yathā dravya artha tā sthitāḥ jñānam svabhāvāt dravya artham karma tvāt ca ātmanaḥ tathā bhavatu evam tathā api iṣṭa rūpa siddhiḥ kutas matā śabdaḥ tad artha kārya tve tau  ajñānasya niṣṭhitaḥ jñānāt jñeyasya kaḥ anyaḥ arthaḥ sva rūpa pratibhāsanāt adṛṣṭa kalpanā yuktāḥ na ca dṛṣṭasya saṃbhave tasmāt yathā prokṣaṇa ādi vidheḥ vrīhiṣu gamyate adṛṣṭā saṃskṛtiḥ jñānavidhe rūpam tathā ātmanaḥ ukta uttaram idam yasmāt arūpeṇa api bhāsakam jñānam pradarśitam na syāt anyathā tad vidheḥ phalam layaḥ nivartyaḥ na tathā vikṣepaḥ hi yathāsukhaḥ laya vikṣepa bhedaḥ ca na syāt agraha mātrake nanu arūpa avabhāse syāt adṛṣṭa phala kalpanā rūpa avabhāse dṛṣṭam tu phalam tad siddhi lakṣaṇam na dṛṣṭa mātrāt dṛṣṭa artham puruṣa artha anugam tu tad na bhidyate ca tad bhāvaḥ rūpā rūpa avabhāsayoḥ yathā prapañca śūnya tve tattve jñāna avabhāsite duḥkha karma kleśa hānam atattve api tathā bhavet etad ca atra bhavet yuktam pratyakṣa ādi avirodhataḥ anaupacārikā arthāḥ ca syuḥ karma viṣayaḥ yatas jānātiḥ tattva bodhe ced na mithyā ādi viśeṣaṇāt na ātmā jñātaḥ tathā syāt ced na tad rūpā vidhānataḥ pramāṇa antara siddha tve natarām anyathā yadi śabda arpitena rūpeṇa tasya jñānam vidhīyate atattvena api tena asya vijñānam avakalpate jñānam yadi ca dṛṣṭa artham prāptam dṛṣṭatas eva tad ātma rūpa vijijñāsaḥ rajijñāsoḥ vṛthā eva tad tad upāyaḥ na ced jñātaḥ tasya eva astu vidhiḥ tatas avaghāta ādi vidhayaḥ na adṛṣṭa sparśa varjitāḥ alaukikam idam jñānam atas yadi vidhīyate na rūpa siddhaye tāvat yatas kartavyatā vidheḥ kartavyatā ca dṛṣṭa arthe dṛṣṭāt eva prasiddhyati alaukike artha rūpe atas śabdena pratipādite jñāne jñeya abhyupāya tvāt svayam eva unmukhaḥ pumān tad upāye tu avijñāte vidhinā sa pravartyatām niyoga anupraveśaḥ atas vihanti eva pramāṇa tām bhūtarthaḥ iti na upāsyaḥ bhūta artha gatim icchatā atas eva na vijñāna saṃtāna vidhi niṣṭhatā tatra api pūrvavat na syāt svarūpa avagati pramā svarūpa niṣṭhāt śabdāt tu pramitasya vacaḥ antarāt upāsana vidhānam syāt prāpteḥ tad api vā vṛthā abhyāsena pratyayasya prakarṣasya abhivīkṣaṇāt tasmāt bhavati akārye arthe vedāntānām pramāṇa tā yathā anya māna vyatibheda muktā matiḥ pramāṇa tvam upaiti kārye apauruṣeya āgama labdha janmā tathā eva siddhe nirapekṣa bhāvāt nanu anvita padārtha tvāt vākya arthasya padārtha tām aprāpya na syāt vākya arthaḥ padārtha tve anya mānakaḥ sāmānyena padārtha tve siddhe asādhāraṇaiḥ guṇaiḥ śakyā pūrva viśeṣasya loka vat pratipādanā sarva pratyaya vedye vā brahma rūpe vyavasthite prapañcasya pravilayaḥ śabdena pratipādyate pravilīna prapañcena tad rūpeṇa na gocaraḥ māna antarasya iti matam āmnāya eka nibandhanam nanu ca apuruṣa artha tvam bhūta niṣṭhe prasajyate pramāṇasya na ca etāvat puruṣa artha eka niṣṭhatā api ca ajñānataḥ śokī duḥkhī jīvaḥ prakāśate tad nivṛttiḥ ca vijñānam puruṣa arthaḥ svayam matam kasmiṃścid arthe vijñāte kutūhalavatām nṛṇām ākulānām jñāna janma puruṣa artha tva saṃmatam akṣa ādau ca yathārūpa niṣṭhe na apuruṣa artha tā na ca pravṛtti niṣṭha tvam tathā asmin kena vāryate jñeyasya puruṣa artha tvam tatra ced atra tad samam puruṣa arthaḥ svayam brahma praśāntam vijarā ādikam atas tad tvam asi iti evam anuśiṣyāya śiṣyate para pravṛtteḥ aṅga tvam pratyakṣa ādeḥ matam yadi atra api sākṣātkaraṇa pravṛttau  aṅgatā iṣya tām eṣā vedānta tattva pravicaya catura prasphurat nyāya tejāḥ mārgam mukteḥ nirundhat nibiḍam api tamaḥ  anādi nirdārayantī sadyas prakṣālayantī ghanam api jagatām tīrtha dustarka paṅkam vyākhyāta utkhāta viśvā śiva niratiśaya śreyase brahma siddhiḥ
