vā tasmāt jīvataḥ tattva jñānam utpadyate iti siddham ātma jñānāt eva muktiḥ iti siddhā jīvanmuktiḥ sa yaḥ ha vai tat paramam brahma
veda brahmā eva bhavati brahma vid āpnoti param iti ādi śrutibhyaḥ nanu jñānasya mokṣa phalaka tve śrutiṣu sahakāri antaram pratīyate
iti ced na jñāna mātrasya śravaṇāt bhidyate hṛdaya granthiḥ iti na anyaḥ panthāḥ vidyate ayanāya iti sahakāri antara pratiṣedhāt ca
nanu śrutiḥ eva maraṇa sahakāriṇaḥ jñānāt mokṣam darśayati tasya tāvat eva ciram yāvat na vimokṣye atha sampatsye iti na
pūrva utpannasya jñānasya cira pravṛtta tvāt maraṇa kāle tasya saṃnidhāpayitum aśakteḥ tad kālam eva utpannāt jñāna antarāt muktiḥ iti ced 
na yat eva bhagavān veda tat eva me brūhi iti ācāryavān puruṣaḥ vedaḥ iti ādi śruti paryālocanayā prathama jñānāt eva mukteḥ śrūyamāṇa tvāt 
etena vacana antaram anugṛhītam bhavati jīvan= eva hi vidvān harṣa marṣābhyām vimucyate iti na ca na ha vai sa śarīrasya sataḥ
priya apriyayoḥ apahatiḥ asti iti śruti antara virodhaḥ vācyaḥ śruti antarasya sāmānya viṣaya tvāt atra vidvān iti viśeṣa nirdeśāt tasya
tāvat eva ciram iti śrutiḥ tyaktā syāt iti ced na vyavasthayā upapatteḥ tathā hi muktiḥ khalu svābhāvikī sarveṣām na sā jñānena
janyate kim tarhi avidyā timira tirohitāyāḥ mukteḥ timira mātram nirākriyate tat ca prathama jñānena eva nirākṛtam tathā api
avidyā kāryasya dehasya avināśāt punar punar mahā andhakāra vat utsāritam api tiraskaroti tasya tiraskāra pratibhāsasya
deha vicchedāt vicchedaḥ bhavati evam ca sati prācīnam eva mahā andhakāra utsāraṇam jñāna mātra nibandhanam avatiṣṭhate yathā sūrya udayena
mahā timira utsāraṇe kṛte api chatra ādi kṛtasya timira ābhāsasya chatra ādi vigame vigamaḥ tatas ca prācīnam eva mahā timira utsāraṇam
sūrya udaya mātra nibandhanam avatiṣṭhate iti tasmāt na jñāna antarāt muktiḥ api tu pūrva utpanna jñānāt eva muktiḥ iti siddham nanu
yadi pāramārthikam advaitam mithyājñāna vijṛmbhitaḥ ca prapañcaḥ iti śruti arthaḥ avadhāritaḥ tat katham sati api bādhake
prapañca anuvṛttiḥ na hi sati eva śuktikā jñāne rajata ādi prapañcaḥ anurtate ucyate na iti na iti na hi etasmāt iti na iti anyat param asti na iha
nānā asti kiñcanaḥ ityādi vākya sahitāt tattvam asya ādi vākyāt prapañca vilaya dvāreṇa asandigdham abādhitam ca advaita jñānam
tāvat utpadyate na ca tat prapañca pratyayena bādhyate iti yuktam tad pravilayena eva utpatteḥ yat punaruktam katham
prapañca pratyaya anuvṛttiḥ iti atra ucyate dvividham hi bādhakam bhavati yathā sati eva mithyājñāna hetu bhūte pitta ādau jāgrati eva pītaḥ
śaṅkhaḥ iti jñāne nimitta antarāt na ayam pītaḥ iti mithyājñāna kāraṇa apagame vā yathā manda āloka prabhavasya śuktikā ra jata jñānasya
mahati āloke na idam rajatam iti jñānam tadvat iha api pītaḥ śaṅkhaḥ iti jñāna bādhaka vat sati eva mithyājñāne hetu bhūte śarīre
prapañca pratyayasya bādhakam advaita jñānam utpadyate atas eva sva kāraṇāt bādhitam api prapañca jñānam pīta śaṅkha jñāna vat punar punar 
jāyate iti nanu dehasya api prapañca antargata tvāt ucchittiḥ eva prasajyate iti ced na prārabdha karma vaśāt anuvṛtteḥ karmaṇaḥ ca
kulāla cakra bhramaṇa vat saṃskārāt anuvṛttiḥ iti siddhā jīvanmuktiḥ iti karma saṃskāra kṣayaḥ ca dehapāte sati sarvasya eva
prapañca pratyayasya api pravilayaḥ karma antarāṇām ca jñānena kṣapita tvāt deha antara anutpattiḥ iti parama muktiḥ evam ca sati
jñāna mātrāt muktiḥ iti pratipādanāt eva karmaṇaḥ mukti hetu tvam apāstam veditavyam tathā hi na tāvat kevalāt karmaṇaḥ mukti śravaṇāt 
na api tatas eva jñāna sahitāt aśruteḥ eva nanu tam vidyā karmaṇī samanvārabhete pūrva prajñā ca iti vidyā karmaṇoḥ sahabhāvaḥ śrūyate
satyam saṃsāra viṣayam tat śravaṇam na mukti viṣayam iti yāvat jīvam agnihotram juhoti iti ādi codanā prāptānām
nitya naimittika karmaṇām jñānasya ca arthāt samuccayaḥ iti ced na viniyojaka pramāṇa abhāvāt tam etam veda anuvacanena brāhmaṇā
vividiṣanti yajñena dānena tapasā anāśakena iti atra tṛtīyā śrutiḥ viniyojikā iti ced na vividiṣā sambandhāt karmaṇām
jñāna artha tva pratīteḥ mokṣa artha tvam na avagamyate iti kim ca na hi jñānam ajñāna nivṛttau upakārakam apekṣate utpannāt eva tasmāt 
ajñāna nivṛtteḥ avaśyam bhāvāt tathā ca śrutiḥ tam eva viditvā atimṛtyum eti na anyaḥ panthāḥ vidyate ayanāya iti api ca yadi
karma phalam mokṣaḥ bhavet tadā anitya tvam prasajyate ghaṭa ādi vat svarga ādi vat ca iti amum eva artham śrutiḥ api āha tat yathā iha karma citaḥ 
lokaḥ kṣīyate evam eva amutra puṇya citaḥ lokaḥ kṣīyate iti agni hotra ādi tu tad kāryāya eva iti sūtrakāreṇa paramparayā karmaṇām
mukti hetu tvam abhihitam prayāja ādi vat atas jñāna artha tvena karmaṇām upayogaḥ asti eva jñāna utpatteḥ tu param karmaṇām upayoga abhāve api
loka saṅgraha artham anuṣṭhānam kartavyam eva iti sarvam samañjasam nanu katham ekaḥ eva ātmā yugapad anekām buddhim adhitiṣṭhati na
hi ekaḥ eva aśvasādī yugapad anekān aśvān adhitiṣṭhan= upalabhyate krameṇa tu adhiṣṭhānam yuktam tat ca iha na asti yugapad eva
sarva buddhīnām sva vyāpāre pravṛtti darśanāt anadhiṣṭhitānām ca pravṛtti asambhavāt ca atas na ekaḥ ātmā iti atas āha yathā yena
prakāśaka tva prakāreṇa raviḥ ādityaḥ ekaḥ eva anekeṣām cakṣuṣām prakāśakaḥ yugapad eva anekāni cakṣūṃṣi adhitiṣṭhati na ca krameṇa
ekaikasmai cakṣuṣe prakāśyam prakāśīkaroti tathā tena eva prakāreṇa ekaḥ ca asau prabodhaḥ ca eka prabodhaḥ saḥ adhiṣṭhātā anekā dhiyaḥ 
buddhīḥ yugapad adhitiṣṭhati na krameṇa ekaikasyai dhiyai prakāśyam prakāśīkaroti yaḥ saḥ aham ātmā iti sambandhaḥ nanu astu tarhi
raviḥ eva buddhīnām prerakaḥ adhiṣṭhātā kim ātma abhyupagamena tathā ca śrutiḥ dhiyaḥ yaḥ naḥ pracodayāt iti atas āha vivasvatā sūryeṇa
prabhātam prakāśitam rūpam yathā yena prakāreṇa akṣam cakṣuḥ pragṛhṇāti prakarṣeṇa jānāti na ābhātam na aprakāśitam andhakāre
ghaṭa ādi anupalambhāt evam vivasvān api ekaḥ tathā tena eva prakāreṇa yadābhātaḥ yena adhitiṣṭhitaḥ san ābhāsayati adhitiṣṭhati akṣam
yathā vivasvān adhiṣṭhātā tathā vivasvataḥ api adhiṣṭhātā yaḥ saḥ aham ātmā iti sambandhaḥ sa ca ahambuddhīnām adhiṣṭhātā śrutyā tu
cakṣuḥ adhiṣṭhātṛ tvam abhipretya vivasvataḥ buddhi preraka tvam abhihitam yasmāt vivasvat adhiṣṭhitam cakṣuḥ buddhi vṛddhim utpādayati
adhiṣṭhātuḥ ātmanaḥ vā sva rūpam abhipretya uktaḥ tathā ca sūryaḥ ātmā jagataḥ tasthuṣaḥ ca iti nanu api tarhi prakāśa antareṇa eva
adhiṣṭhātavyam na tasya sva prakāśa tvāt na anyat atas asti draṣṭā iti ca śrutyā tad itara pratiṣedhāt ca kim ca yathā yena prakāreṇa
ādityaḥ ekaḥ apsu vāriṣu calāsu sthirāsu ca anekaḥ api nānā api ekaḥ san pratibhāsate ananvac vibhāvya svarūpaḥ anu paścāt añcati
gacchati iti anvak na anvak ananvac ananugataḥ iti yāvat yat vā ananvaktvena vibhāvyam svarūpam yasya sa tathā uktaḥ evam
bahu vrīhi samāsam kṛtvā paścāt nañsamāsaḥ tatas ca ayam arthaḥ bhavati na vāriṣu raviḥ anugataḥ bhavati kim tarhi tathā eva nabhasi
dedīpyamānaḥ bhrāntyā vāriṣu dṛśyate iti arthaḥ evam ekaḥ ātmā calāsu prabhinnāsu nānā bhūtāsu dhīṣu buddhiṣu anekaḥ san
ananvac vibhāvya svarūpaḥ na buddhīḥ anugataḥ bhavati kim tarhi pṛthak eva dedīpyate yaḥ saḥ aham ātmā iti arthaḥ kim ca ghanena meghena
channā tirohitā dṛṣṭiḥ darśanam yasya saḥ ghana= channa dṛṣṭiḥ puruṣaḥ ghana= channam arkam ādityam yathā yena
ghana= channa tva prakāreṇa manyate jānāti niṣprabham prabhā rahitam aprakāśa svabhāvam iti yāvat mūḍhaḥ manyate
ghana= channa dṛṣṭi tvāt prakāśa svabhāvam api ravim aprakāśam paśyati iti āha ati mūḍhaḥ iti ati mūrkha tvāt sva ātmanaḥ 
dṛṣṭi vighātam agaṇayan sūryam eva aprakāśam manyate pāda pūraṇe cakāraḥ tathā tena prakāreṇa avidyā āt channa dṛṣṭiḥ
buddhim ātma tvena gṛhītvā tad gata duḥkha ādikam ātmani adhyāropya baddhaḥ iva ābhāti yaḥ mūḍha dṛṣṭeḥ saḥ aham ātmā iti sambandhaḥ kim ca
samasteṣu niravaśeṣeṣu prapañca ātmakeṣu sat ā ātmanā anusyūtam anugatam vyāptam evam hi nānā samastāni vastūni prapañca ātmakāni yam
sat rūpam na spṛśanti kutas viyadvat ākāśam iva sadā sarvadā śuddham nirmalam rāga ādi doṣa rahitam accha svarūpam amṛta rūpam yat 
param brahma saḥ aham ātmā iti sambandhaḥ vyutpāditam artham upasaṃharati upādhau sati upādhi bheda sambandhe sati yathā bheda tā bheda
eva bheda tā sva arthe tat sat maṇīnām viśuddha maṇīnām sphaṭika ādīnām lohita kṛṣṇa ādi bhedena bheda tā bhedaḥ tathā buddhi bhedeṣu
