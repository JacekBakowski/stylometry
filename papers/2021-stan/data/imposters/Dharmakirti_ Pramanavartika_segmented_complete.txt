kāraṇāt  kārya saṃsiddhiḥ svabhāva antargamāt  iyam hetu prabheda ākhyāyena darśita udāhṛtiḥ pṛthak  eka upalambha anubhavāt  idam na upalabhe iti buddheḥ  upalabhe vā iti kalpikāyāḥ samudbhavaḥ viśeṣaḥ  gamyate arthānām viśiṣṭāt  eva vedanāt  tathābhūta ātma saṃvittiḥ  bheda dhī hetuḥ  asya ca tasmāt  svatas  dhiyoḥ  bheda siddhi s tābhyām tad  arthayoḥ anyathā hi  anavasthānāt  bhedaḥ sidhyet  na kasyacid  viśiṣṭa rūpa anubhavān na atas  anya anya nirākriyā tad  viśiṣṭa upalambhaḥ  atas  tasya api  anupalambhanam tasmāt  anupalambhaḥ  ayam svayam pratyakṣataḥ gataḥ sva  mātra vṛtteḥ  gamaka s tad  abhāva vyavasthiteḥ anyathā arthasya na astitvam gamyate anupalambhataḥ upalambhasya nāstitvam anyena iti  anavasthitiḥ adṛśye niścaya ayogāt  sthitiḥ  anyatra vāryate yathā aliṅgaḥ  anya sattveṣu vikalpa ādiḥ  na sidhyati aniścaya phalā hi  eṣā na alam vyāvṛtti sādhane ādyā adhikriyate hetoḥ  niścayena eva sādhane tasyāḥ svayam prayogeṣu svarūpam vā prayujyate artha bādhana rūpam vā bhāve bhāvāt  abhāvataḥ anyonya bheda siddheḥ  vā dhruva bhāva vināśavat  pramāṇa antara bādhād vā sa apekṣa dhruva bhāvavat  hetv antara samūhasya saṃnidhau niyamaḥ kutas  bhāva hetu bhavatve kim pāraṃparya pariśramaiḥ nāśanam janayitvā anyam sa hetuḥ  tasya nāśanaḥ tam eva naśvaram bhāvam janayet  yadi kim bhavet  ātma upakārakaḥ kaḥ syāt  tasya siddha ātmanaḥ sataḥ na ātma upakārakaḥ kaḥ syāt  tena yaḥ samapekṣyate anapekṣaḥ  ca kim bhāvaḥ atathābhūtaḥ kadācana yathā na kṣepa bhāg iṣṭaḥ sa eva udbhūta nāśakaḥ kṣaṇam api  anapekṣa tve bhāvaḥ bhāvasya na iti ced  bhāvaḥ
