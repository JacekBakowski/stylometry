akurvat iṣṭam yadi vā asya kārakam na kiṃcid anyat nanu na asti akārakam sataḥ aviśeṣāt asataḥ ca sat cyutau tulā antayoḥ yadvat aniścayāt na hi na ced saḥ iṣṭaḥ sat asat viparyayaḥ katham bhavaḥ syāt sat asat vyavasthitau vibhaktam etad dvayam api avasthitam na janma tasmāt ca manaḥ hi kasyacid atha abhyupetya api bhavam tava icchataḥ bravīmi na arthaḥ tava ceṣṭitena me na hāna vṛddhī na yatas svatas asataḥ bhavaḥ anyatas vā yadi vā astitā tayoḥ dhruvāḥ hi anityāḥ ca na ca anya yoginaḥ mithas ca kāryam na ca teṣu yujyate atas na kasya api hi kiṃcid iṣyate svayam hi tattvam na nirukti gocaram samam tu tasmāt satatam vibhāta vat dvayāt vimuktam sat asat vikalpitāt nirīkṣya yuktyā śrutitaḥ ca buddhimān aśeṣa nirvāṇam upaiti dīpa vat avedyam ekam yad ananya vedinām kutārkikāṇām ca suvedyam anyathā nirīkṣya ca ittham tu aguṇa grahaḥ aguṇam na yāti moham graha doṣa muktitaḥ atas anyathā na graha nāśaḥ iṣyate vimoha buddheḥ grahaḥ eva kāraṇam grahaḥ api ahetuḥ hi analaḥ tu anindhanaḥ yathā praśāntim paramām tathā vrajet vimathya veda udadhitaḥ samuddhṛtam suraiḥ mahā abdheḥ  tu yathā mahātmabhiḥ tathā amṛtam jñānam idam hi yaiḥ purā namaḥ gurubhyaḥ param īkṣitam ca yaiḥ iti jvara nāśa prakaraṇam
