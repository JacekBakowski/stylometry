avirodhayoḥ vikalpā sambhavāt samuccaya vidhānāt avirodhaḥ eva iti ced na saha sambhava anupapatteḥ krameṇa eka āśraye syātām vidyā vidye iti ced na vidyā utpattau tad āśraye avidyā anupapatteḥ na hi agniḥ uṣṇaḥ prakāśaḥ ca iti vijñāna utpattau na api saṃśayaḥ ajñānam vā iti śoka moha ādi asambhava śruteḥ avidyā sambhavāt tad upādānasya karmaṇaḥ api anupapattim avocāma iti āpekṣikam amṛtam vidyā śabdena paramātma vidyā grahaṇe ityādinā dvāra mārga yācanam anupapannam syāt  tasmāt yathāvyākhyātaḥ eva mantrāṇām arthaḥ iti uparamyate
