anālambanāḥ śiṣṭā daśa dharmadhātu eka deśaḥ ca kati sa vitarkāḥ manaḥ dhātuḥ manaḥ vijñāna dhātuḥ dharmadhātu eka deśaḥ ca kati niṣpannāḥ pañca ādhyātmikāḥ caturṇām eka deśaḥ kati aniṣpannāḥ śiṣṭā daśa caturṇām eka deśaḥ ca kati sa bhāgāḥ ādhyātmikāḥ pañca rūpiṇaḥ sva vijñāna sahita viṣaya sadṛśa tām upādāya kati tad sa bhāgāḥ ta eva sva vijñāna virahita sva anvaya sadṛśa tām upādāya dhātu uddyeśa nirdeśakaḥ tṛtīya adhikaraḥ pariniṣṭitaḥ ācārya vasubandhu viracit pañca skandha prakaraṇam samāptam pañca skandha prakaraṇam śāstriṇā śāntibhikṣuṇā bhoṭa anuvādam āgamya saṃskṛte punar uddhṛtamyat atra sukṛtam sarva tad ācāryasya dhīmataḥ yad  kukṛtam tad āgaḥ  me kṣantavyam sādhu sūribhiḥ
