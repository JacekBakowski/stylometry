yathā nityaḥ śabdaḥ  amūrta tvāt  karmavat  paramāṇuvat  ghaṭavat  iti ete dṛṣṭānta ābhāsāḥ sādhya sādhana dharmaubhaya vikalāḥ tathā sandigdha sādhya dharma ādayaḥ  ca yathā rāga ādimān ayam vacanāt  rathyā puruṣavat maraṇa dharmaḥ  ayam puruṣaḥ  rāga ādimat tvāt  rathyā puruṣavat asarvajñaḥ  ayam rāga ādima tvād rathyā puruṣavat  iti tathā ananvayaḥ  apradarśita anvayaḥ  ca yathā yaḥ  uktā sa rāga ādimān iṣṭa puruṣavat  anityaḥ śabdaḥ kṛtaka tvāt  ghaṭavat  iti tathā viparīta anvayaḥ yad  anityam tad  kṛtakam iti sādharmyeṇa dṛṣṭānta doṣāḥ vaidharmyeṇa api paramāṇuvat  karmavat  ākāśavat  iti sādhyādi vyatirekiṇaḥ tathā sandigdha sādhya vyatireka ādayaḥ yathā asarva jñāḥ kapilādayaḥ  anāptā vā avidyamāna sarvajña tāāptatā liṅga bhūta pramāṇa atiśaya śāsana tvāt  iti atra vaidharmya audāharaṇam yaḥ sarvajña āptaḥ  vā sa jyotiḥ jñāna ādikam upadiṣṭavān yathā ṛṣabha vardhamāna ādiḥ  iti tatra asarvajña tā anāptayoḥ sādhya dharmayoḥ sandigdhaḥ  vyatirekaḥ sandigdha sādhana vyatirekaḥ  yathā na trayī vidā brāhmaṇena grāhya vacanaḥ kaścid  vivakṣitaḥ puruṣaḥ  rāga ādimat tvāt  iti atra vaidharmya audāharaṇam ye grāhya vacanā na te rāgādimantaḥ tad  yathā gautamādayaḥ  dharma śāstrāṇām praṇetāra iti gautama ādibhyaḥ  rāga ādimattvasya sādhana dharmasya vyāvṛttiḥ sandigdhā sandigdhaubhaya vyatirekaḥ  yathā avīta rāgāḥ kapila ādayaḥ parigraha agraha yogāt  iti atra vaidharmyeṇa udāharaṇam yaḥ  vīta rāgaḥ  na tasya parigraha āgrahaḥ yathā ṛṣabha ādeḥ  iti ṛṣabha ādeḥ  avīta rāgatva parigraha āgraha yogayoḥ sādhya sādhana dharmayoḥ sandigdhaḥ  vyatirekaḥ avyatirekaḥ  yathā avīta rāgaḥ  ayam vaktṛ tvāt vaidharmyeṇa udāharaṇam yatra avīta rāga tvam na asti na sa vaktā yathā upalakhaṇḍa iti yadi  api  upala khaṇḍāt  ubhayam vyāvṛttam tathā api sarvaḥ  vīta rāgaḥ  na vaktā iti vyāptyā vyatirekaa siddheḥ  avyatirekaḥ apradarśita vyatirekaḥ  yathā anityaḥ śabdaḥ kṛtaka tvāt  ākāśavat  iti vaidharmyeṇa viparīta vyatirekaḥ  yathā yad  akṛtakam tad  nityam bhavati iti na hi  ebhiḥ  dṛṣṭānta ābhāsaiḥ hetoḥ sāmānya lakṣaṇam sa pakṣa eva sattvam vipakṣe ca sarvatra asattvam eva niścayena śakyam darśayitum viśeṣa lakṣaṇam vā tad  arthāpattyā eṣām nirāsaḥ draṣṭavyaḥ dūṣaṇā nyūna tāādi uktiḥ ye pūrvam nyūna tāādayaḥ sādhana doṣā uktā as teṣām udbhāvanam dūṣaṇam tena paraiṣṭa artha siddhi pratibandhāt  dūṣaṇa ābhāsāḥ  tu jātayaḥ abhūta doṣa
